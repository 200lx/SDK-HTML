Appointment Book Overview

The appointment book data records are of a fixed format. For the appointment book data records, there are several types:

  • Non-repeating appointments

  • Repeating appointments

  • Non-repeating todos

  • Repeating todos

  • Checked-off Repeating todo

The items that occur on a single day are referred to as single-occurrence items. Repeating items, todos with carry-forward enabled, and appointments/events with more than one consecutive day are called multi-occurrence items.

The appointment book has two special records of record type 14 (TYPE_USER). Record 0 of TYPE_USER has the Appointment book info structure. This holds information about the data records and appointment book defaults.
